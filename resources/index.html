About our company
Since the dawn of time, whenever mankind stumbled upon something unknown, two questions popped into our minds. "Can we eat it?" was the first, and if the answer was "No" there would come the second one: "Can we use it for sex?"

Now that we live in a time when people are actively encouraged to explore their sexuality, our mission, nay, our drive, is to help you address your urges in an healthy and safe way.

It is generally accepted that Disney is a synonym of a good childhood and if you got here by your own, most likely you had a crush or an admiration for one or more of its characters. Maybe when you became aware that there was more to it than just peeing or maybe when you were watching a cartoon with your kids and you realized that Woody and Buzz are more than an unlikely duo. Honestly, we don't care. And we don't judge. That's not our job here. Our job is to help you help yourself and, who knows, your relationship. You might just want to fulfill that hole inside you where your childhood used to be, or just have a laugh with your parthner after the flame of the youth is gone, or even just share a laugh with someone special. When the road looks rough ahead and you're miles and miles from your nice warm bed, just remember what your old pals said. You've got a friend in us.

All our products are made from recycled materials and following responsible economic practices. No child nor animal labour were involved in the process chain.
